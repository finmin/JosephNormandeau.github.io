03 / Projects

Engineering Work

Aerodynamics Lab
Wind Tunnel Airfoil Analysis

Processed wind tunnel pressure data for NACA airfoils using MATLAB to analyze lift, drag, and aerodynamic performance. Compared experimental results against theoretical models, validating boundary-layer and potential-flow predictions.

MATLAB Data Analysis NACA Airfoils
High-Speed Aerodynamics Lab
Shock Tube Experiment

Measured shock wave propagation using pressure transducers to experimentally determine shock speed. Analyzed time-series pressure data in MATLAB and correlated findings with normal shock relations and Rankine–Hugoniot conditions.

MATLAB Pressure Transducers Shock Dynamics
Aerospace Structures Lab
Structural Mechanics Testing

Conducted tensile testing of aluminum and steel specimens to failure. Extracted material properties including Young's modulus from strain-gauge measurements, comparing results to published handbook values for engineering metals.

Tensile Testing Strain Gauges Materials Science
Orbital Mechanics
Orbital Transfer Simulation

Developed MATLAB scripts to numerically solve Kepler's equation and model Hohmann transfer trajectories between circular orbits. Computed delta-V requirements, transfer times, and visualized orbital paths for mission-planning scenarios.

MATLAB Kepler's Equation Orbital Mechanics
